Why do we miss sometimes?

There are multiple reasons why our model may misclassify an image.
(1) Our model was trained on a dataset with specific requirements (please see For Best Results section). Because of this, it may misclassify some images that do not meet the requirements.
(2) We handlabeled each image, so mistakes during labeling could contribute to the model making mistakes.
(3) Our model was trained with individual ASL letters and not letters in words.
(4) The model was made in a limited time frame of 3 weeks.
